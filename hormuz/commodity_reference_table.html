Notes
"Strait share" = percentage of global seaborne trade in that commodity transiting the Strait of Hormuz. Severity bands: Extreme >25% High 15–25% Medium 8–15% Moderate <8%. "Conf." = confidence in the quoted percentage: High = directly stated by IEA, UN, or equivalent authority; Med = derived from multiple consistent sources; Low = estimated from partial data or industry reports. Methanol and MEG percentages carry the lowest confidence and should be cited as estimates. All source links are clickable. Data as of early April 2026.
